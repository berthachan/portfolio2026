Discovery & Alignment

Aura had strong brand equity but a broken funnel. Before any design work, we needed to understand exactly where and why users were abandoning the experience.

Funnel Analysis

The "Five Whys" Framework

Session recordings showed users spending 3–4 minutes on the product page before leaving — not confused, but unconvinced. The gap was trust, not comprehension.

I analysed FullStory session recordings, ran moderated usability tests with 8 existing customers, and conducted a heuristic audit of the existing app against Nielsen's principles.

Users would scroll repeatedly on the PDP but leave without adding to cart. Exit surveys pointed to three specific trust gaps: ambiguity around limited-edition status, unreliable sizing information, and no way to quickly compare products.

These friction points mapped directly to revenue loss. A 10% improvement to PDP-to-cart conversion would represent significant monthly ARR growth at Aura's volume.

I defined three core design principles for the new experience: confidence (users know what they're buying), clarity (information is easy to find), and delight (the brand shines through).
