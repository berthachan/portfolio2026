The Winning Process

Check-
list

A systematic approach to solving complex product challenges, ensuring no detail is overlooked from discovery to handoff.

Iterative Loops
01. Discovery & Alignment

Establishing the core problem statement. Conducting stakeholder interviews, defining user personas, and mapping out initial user journeys to ensure business goals align with user needs.

02. Information Architecture

Structuring the content. Creating sitemaps, user flows, and low-fidelity wireframes to establish the structural foundation before visual design begins.

03. Interaction & Visuals

Translating wireframes into high-fidelity screens. Applying or creating design systems, establishing typography hierarchy, and designing micro-interactions.

04. Prototyping & Testing

Building interactive models in Figma to test usability. Conducting moderated testing sessions and iterating based on empirical user feedback.

05. Handoff & Documentation

Preparing developer-ready specs. Annotating edge cases, creating component documentation, and ensuring a smooth transition from design to engineering.
